How It Works

The process of selling your car with Cardeal is easy and straightforward. Our employees will keep you informed so you will understand what happens next.

1

Fill Out Form

Provide accurate information about your car (i.e., VIN, year, make, model, mileage, condition, accidents).

2

Get an Instant Offer

Receive an offer on your car that is valid for 7 days.

3

Provide Access to Car

Deliver your car to the nearest Cardeal location or have us pick it up.

4

Submit Title

Provide the title to verify your ownership of the vehicle.

5

Car Inspection

Cardeal will inspect your car to verify its condition.

6

Accept Offer

If everything checks out, choose cash or apply the value towards a trade-in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Cardeal will buy "any" car even if it has a blown engine. At Cardeal, we want to offer the highest value, quick payment for cars, convenience, and a secure transaction. If you sell us your car, you are not obligated to buy your next car from us although we promise that if you do, you will get a great deal.

Prices are calculated based on the current market value of your car and its condition.

Cardeal is a free service. There are no charges for evaluations and offers. If you sell a car with Cardeal, we pay for pick up and towing. We have no hidden costs.

Cardeal has a branch in every state. Therefore, we can usually pick up your vehicle within 24 to 48 hours after you accept our offer.

When filling out the online form, you will be asked to enter your Vehicle Identification Number (VIN). Cardeal also requires a title in the seller's name. We cannot buy your vehicle without a valid, signed title from the registered vehicle owner.

If you have a damaged, wrecked, or non-running car, Cardeal can help you get rid of it. We'll give you a fair market offer for your car that's tailored to your vehicle's conditions and local market demands.
